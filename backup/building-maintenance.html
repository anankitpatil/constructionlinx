    Building maintenance can be divided into two categories -
    unplanned and planned. Whichever approach you adopt we can help estimate, carryout out and provide a suitable maintenance solution. In unplanned maintenance there is no organised arrangement to follow and everything is carried out as a reaction to an emergency situation This usually results in untimely breakdowns, frustration and unexpected costs.

    Unplanned maintenance suggests that the individual in charge of building maintenance accepts no responsibility for the upkeep of the premises and the inevitable consequences. It could put the person responsible for maintenance at risk of not meeting their statutory requirements and is not recommended as a technique to be adopted.

    We help every customer have a planned maintenance approach to their premises. By becoming your preferred maintenance provider we can help you prioritise projects or as a minimum offer you our basic “as you need us” service.

    Planned projects include everything from repainting windows and clearing gutters to building extensions to buildings or relaying car parks. We can help you plan, budget and organise your maintenance programme to suit the funds available and at a time to suit.

    Our success results from...

    • Hand selecting professionals covering all trades, who adhere to our precise standards & values
    • Efficient organising and project management of all building trades
    • Understanding different maintenance approaches we can help maximise any capital budget.
