    Construction Linx Local

    Construction Linx Local is the complete building maintenance and construction company. We cover Cheshire, Shropshire and Staffordshire and specialise in the building and maintenance of industrial properties, commercial businesses and public buildings.

    We offer every trade skill and maintenance service. From the construction of a new warehouse to the jetting of drains and maintenance of schools or industrial estates, Construction Linx is the only building company you will ever need.

    We are available for individual projects or on a contract basis, employ only reliable and expertly trained personnel and stake our reputation on quality workmanship and project management.

    Appearances are important to both potential clients and customers. A safe and smart work environment boosts the morale of your employees. Construction Linx is the strategic partner to many successful businesses that realise the importance of these values.

    We take the strain out of the construction and maintenance of your business and leave you to ensure it stays successful.
