    We can advise on different approaches to maintenance projects to suit you and your premises. Our maintenance plans are the most popular choice for businesses such as yours. It makes budgeting for those planned and unplanned projects easy, so you always know you are covered and what the costs will be.

    We have two levels of maintenance plan – Platinum and Platinum Plus.
    Once we have surveyed your site we’ll be able to tailor a package to suit your budget and give you the confidence that your premises are covered so that you can concentrate on running your business. Whichever package you choose we will provide you with a Building Maintenance Log Book. The main purpose of the log book is to provide a summary of all key information about the building or estate which we will manage. This will include site plans, schedules of routine maintenance and emergency contact numbers.
