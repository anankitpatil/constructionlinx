    Single Source Solution

    Construction Linx offer a simple ‘single source’ solution to ensure your premises are maintained to the best possible standards. We have vast experience in ensuring all work is carried out swiftly, efficiently and with the minimum of disturbance to your employees. We take the health and safety of your employees and show meticulous care and respect to the contents of your buildings in the same way as we do with our own people and our own premises.

    There are 3 types of maintenance project

    1. As you need us – Emergency
    2. Maintenance Plans – Platinum or Platinum plus
    3. Planned Projects – Usually ‘one offs’

    Construction Linx cater for all Building Maintenance projects, allowing you to get on with running your business. Think of us as part of your organisation to be there whenever you need us.

    An emergency can range from a burst pipe to a break in or even a piece of routine maintenance that should have been carried out but has been forgotten! We are there to help. We’ll react as quickly as you need us – immediately, next day or to meet that critical deadline.

    As soon as you call your job is scheduled into our work flow system with alerts the relevant people to react. Jobs are fully tracked ensuring nothing is missed and deadlines are always met.

    In many cases emergencies can be overcome by well-planned routine maintenance. Give us a call and we’ll survey your site and recommend a comprehensive maintenance schedule. This can start from just making everything safe and up to date to a more long term maintenance plan to ensure your premises are protected year after year.

    We offer a preventative and cost saving maintenance service. Similar to routine maintenance we will visit your premises and advise you on how you can improve things to protect yourself from unnecessary maintenance costs in the future and to help you save money. This could include replacement windows, insulation, energy saving lighting and UPVC soffits and fascia.

    This covers all major (or minor) maintenance projects that are not part of your plan and are usually seen as a ‘one off’. This can include resurfacing car parks, replacing windows, a new flat roof or complete building projects.

    Our surveyors will meet with you and discuss the appropriate actions that need to be taken and then prepare a comprehensive quotation covering all aspects of the job giving you complete peace of mind.
