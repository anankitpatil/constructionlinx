The best way to maintain your property and buildings is to have a regular schedule of maintenance. Construction Linx can undertake all aspects of maintenance, from regular checks and testing, to actually carrying out the work.

Construction Linx offer a unique Prevention Audit, to identify those things that may go wrong with your buildings or that do not present the quality image your customers and staff demand. It contains;

  • Portable Appliance Testing
  • Fire Risk Assessments
  • Fire Alarms & Equipment testing
  • Electrical Testing
  • Boiler checks
  • Door access systems
  • CCTV Equipment
  • Asbestos Risk Assessment
  • Gutter and roofing maintenance to prevent floods and leaks
  • Ensuring landscaped areas, gardens, car parks and public areas like receptions and meeting rooms are kept immaculate and tidy.

Some of these are tests compulsory by law, others are to enhance your image or increase your health and safety provision. Businesses can either make arrangements themselves or use a reliable partner – that partner is Construction Linx.
